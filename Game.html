Where is data stored?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to any server. Passwords are stored as a salted SHA-256 hash (never plain text). Your score file is AES-256 encrypted and only decrypted after successful login. The AES key is wrapped with RSA-2048 and your RSA private key is protected via PBKDF2.

Where passwords are stored:
Your password is never stored. Only a salted SHA-256 hash is saved. Even if someone reads localStorage, they cannot recover your password.
Where scores are stored:
Scores are stored inside an AES-256-GCM encrypted blob in localStorage. They are unreadable without your password. The key to decrypt is wrapped with RSA-2048, and the RSA private key itself is protected by PBKDF2.
Integrity:
A SHA-256 hash of the encrypted save file is stored. On login, it is recomputed and compared — any tampering is detected.
